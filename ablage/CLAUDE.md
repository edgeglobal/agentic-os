## What's here

Archive. Read-only for active work. Historical snapshots of completed projects, offboarded Mitarbeiter, deprecated processes, and closed Kunden.

## How to use

- Sub-structure mirrors active firma/ folders: `ablage/projekte/`, `ablage/mitarbeiter/`, `ablage/kunden/`, `ablage/prozesse/`
- Move files here when status reaches `completed`, `cancelled`, or `deprecated`. Never delete.
- Frontmatter: add `archived: {YYYY-MM-DD}` when moving a file here.
- Do NOT rewrite or modernize archived content. It is a historical snapshot.
- Search here when looking for prior-art, historical decisions, or reactivating dormant Kunden.
