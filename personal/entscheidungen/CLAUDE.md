## What's here

Personal decision records — career, learning, life. Company-level decisions go in `firma/intelligence/decisions/` (if exists).

## How to use

- Filename: `{YYYY-MM-DD}-{slug}.md`
- Frontmatter: `type: decision`, `status: open | decided | reversed`, `reversibility: high | medium | low`, `created:`
- Body: Context, Options Considered, Decision, Risks, Review-Date
- Only document irreversible/high-stake decisions. Reversible ones not worth the overhead.
