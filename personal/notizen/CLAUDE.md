## What's here

Atomic notes — thinking layer. Each note one concept, flat structure.

## How to use

- Filename: `{slug}.md` (kebab-case, descriptive)
- Frontmatter: `type: note`, `note_type: idea | reference | synthesis | quick`, 2+ tags, `created:`
- Body: links to other notes via `[[wikilinks]]`
- Lifecycle: `note_type: quick` files older than 30 days flagged for cleanup
