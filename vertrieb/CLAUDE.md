## What's here

Sales playbooks, scripts, sequences, targets, pipeline state.

## How to use

- Playbook filename: `playbook-{slug}.md`
- Script filename: `script-{slug}.md`
- Frontmatter: `type: playbook | script | report`, `status:`, `owner:`, `created:`
- Customer references: `[[../kunden/unternehmen/{slug}]]`, `[[../kunden/personen/{slug}]]`
- Sub-Teams (z.B. SDR-Team vs AE-Team): als Subfolder `team-sdr/`, `team-ae/`
