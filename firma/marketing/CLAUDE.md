## What's here

Brand assets, campaigns, content strategy, channel playbooks.

## How to use

- Campaign filename: `kampagne-{slug}.md`
- Content asset filename: `content-{slug}.md`
- Frontmatter: `type: kampagne | content | report | brand-asset`, `status:`, `owner:`, `created:`
- Customer references: `[[../kunden/unternehmen/{slug}]]`, `[[../kunden/personen/{slug}]]`
- Sub-Teams (z.B. Content vs Paid): als Subfolder `team-content/`, `team-paid/`
