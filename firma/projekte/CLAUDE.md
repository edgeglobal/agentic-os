## What's here

Cross-functional projects. One folder per project with own context, tasks, and notes.

## How to use

- Folder per project: `projekte/{slug}/`
- Project README: `projekte/{slug}/README.md` with frontmatter `type: project`, `status: active | paused | completed | cancelled`, `owner:`, `started:`, `deadline:`, `created:`
- Sub-files on demand: `notes/`, `research/`, `tasks/`
- Wikilinks to relevant Kunden, Mitarbeiter, Prozesse
- Completed projects: move to `firma/ablage/projekte/{slug}/`
