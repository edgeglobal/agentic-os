## What's here

Operations, delivery processes, quality assurance, onboarding flows.

## How to use

- Onboarding filename: `onboarding-{slug}.md`
- QA-Checklist filename: `qa-{slug}.md`
- Frontmatter: `type: onboarding | qa | delivery | report`, `status:`, `owner:`, `created:`
- Customer references: `[[../kunden/unternehmen/{slug}]]`, `[[../kunden/personen/{slug}]]`
- Sub-Teams (z.B. Onboarding vs Support): als Subfolder `team-onboarding/`, `team-support/`
