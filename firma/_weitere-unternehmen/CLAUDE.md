## What's here

OPTIONAL: additional companies if the operator runs multiple businesses (Holding-Setup).

Each company has its own subfolder `<biz-slug>/` with the same sub-structure as `firma/`.

## How to use

- Empty by default. Aktiviert via `/playbooks/promote-multi-biz` oder manuelles Anlegen.
- Naming: kebab-case slug (`acme-gmbh`, `beta-holding`)
- Sub-Struktur: copy from `_template/`
- Wikilinks: `[[firma/_weitere-unternehmen/acme-gmbh/ueberblick]]`
