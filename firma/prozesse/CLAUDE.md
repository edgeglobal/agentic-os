## What's here

Standard Operating Procedures, Workflows, Richtlinien.

| Subfolder | Content |
|---|---|
| `sops/` | Step-by-step procedures for repeatable tasks |
| `workflows/` | Multi-role process flows (with diagrams) |
| `richtlinien/` | Company policies, compliance rules |

## How to use

- SOP filename: `SOP-{slug}.md`
- Workflow filename: `WF-{slug}.md`
- Richtlinie filename: `R-{slug}.md`
- Frontmatter: `type: sop | workflow | richtlinie`, `status: draft | active | deprecated`, `owner: {role-or-name}`, `created:`, `updated:`
